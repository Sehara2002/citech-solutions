## The problem

MRCGP Fast track is an institute which provides OSCE and AKT courses for professional doctors worldwide. But they didn't have a website to publish their courses, mock exam informations. Also they didn't have a method to collect payments and register students via online mode.

## What we found in the scoping week

At the analysis stage and scope finalizing stage, We finalized the following requirements for the site.
- A modern stylished view of courses, exams and testimonials.
- A contact page
- An online registration method to courses.
- Online Payment Gateway option.
- Quick inquiry placements.
- A modern stylished and informative landing page.

## What we built

- We successfully built modern stylished website for Dr. Malkanthi. which is hosted on mrcgpfasttrack.com
- We successfully included A Registration portal for new students.
- We integrated commercial bank mastercard payment gateway to collect online payments easily.
- We integrated automated google emailing service.

## Result

Now the doctors easily can find courses and Dr. Malkanthi can easily publish their courses and exam time slots. Also doctors can easily register for courses and exams.
